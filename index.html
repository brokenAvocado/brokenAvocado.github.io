About Me

Welcome to my portfolio!

I'm a senior at the University of Maryland, majoring Aerospace Engineering. For the past Spring term, I interned as a composites manufacturing engineer with Blue Origin, working on various composite components on the New Glenn rocket. For Summer of 2023, I interned with BAE Systems as a Systems Engineer on Trident Submarine operations.

During the school year, I am a lead engineer for UMD Loop, an all undergraduate student engineering team, and I conduct research at the CORE Lab under Dr. Norman Wereley.
